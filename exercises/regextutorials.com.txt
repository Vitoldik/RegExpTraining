1. Floating point numbers
\d\.\d+

2. Years before 1990
.*\(19[1-8]\d\)

3. Hexadecimal colors
#(\d|[A-F]){6}

4. Grayscale colors
#([0-9a-fA-F]{1,2})\1\1

5. Too long lines
^.{30}.+$

6. Remove repeating words
\b(.+)\b\s+\1\b

7. Match HTML tags
<[^<>]+>
